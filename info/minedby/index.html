Mined By

Blocks "Mined by" on explorer.komodo.services

Change your pool signature

With kmd-nomp or z-nomp you need to change your signature.

Open the file: /kmd-nomp/node_modules/stratum-pool/lib/transactions.js

Now find: Buffer('4b4d442d4e4f4d50212068747470733a2f2f6769746875622e636f6d2f6b6f6d6f646f73657276696365732f6b6d642d6e6f6d70', 'hex')]) //KMD-NOMP! https://github.com/komodoservices/kmd-nomp

Encode a HEX string with your name or the name of your pool and/or the URL and change the above line.

Follow this format: YourPoolName https://your.mining.pool

Submit your HEX string

After finishing the above, you need to submit your HEX so we can include it on our explorer.

Create a pull request here: komodoservices/insight-api-komodo

or message @xRobeSx on Komodo Discord.
